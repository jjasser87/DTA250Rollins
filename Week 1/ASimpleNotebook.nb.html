Palmer Penguins Dataset

The palmerpenguins dataset has become a popular alternative to the widely-used iris dataset for data visualization and teaching statistical concepts.

Variables:

  1. species:

    • Type: Factor

    • Levels:

      • Adelie

      • Chinstrap

      • Gentoo

  2. island:

    • Type: Factor

    • Levels:

      • Biscoe

      • Dream

      • Torgersen

  3. bill_length_mm:

    • Type: Numeric

    • Description: Length of the penguins bill in millimeters.

  4. bill_depth_mm:

    • Type: Numeric

    • Description: Depth of the penguins bill in millimeters.

  5. flipper_length_mm:

    • Type: Numeric

    • Description: Length of the penguins flippers in millimeters.

  6. body_mass_g:

    • Type: Numeric

    • Description: Penguins body mass in grams.

  7. sex:

    • Type: Factor

    • Description: Sex of the penguin.

  8. year:

    • Type: Integer

    • Description: Year of observation.

Advantages of Using palmerpenguins over iris:

  • Diversity of Data Types: The dataset includes continuous, categorical, and ordinal variables.

  • Missing Data: Contains some missing values, making it representative of real-world datasets.

  • Ethical Considerations: Avoids controversies associated with Ronald A. Fisher and the iris dataset.

  • Engagement: Penguins are engaging and charismatic!
